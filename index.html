RIver State, Nigeria

Welcome to Rivers State, the vibrant heart of Nigeria's Niger Delta! Nestled by the Atlantic Ocean to the south, and bordered by Imo, Abia, Anambra, Akwa Ibom, Bayelsa, and Delta states, Rivers State is a land woven with a captivating network of rivers and lush mangrove swamps. It's a melting pot of diverse cultures, home to resilient and hospitable people like the Ikwerre, Ijaw, Ogoni, Kalabari, and many more, each contributing to a rich tapestry of over 30 indigenous languages. Explore the bustling capital, Port Harcourt, often dubbed the "Garden City," or delve into natural wonders and cultural gems. Discover landmarks such as the serene Port Harcourt Tourist Beach, the insightful National Museum Port Harcourt, the recreational Port Harcourt Pleasure Park, and the ecological haven of Finima Nature Park on historic Bonny Island. Rivers State offers an unforgettable blend of natural beauty, vibrant traditions, and modern energy.
